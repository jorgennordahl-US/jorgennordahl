Modern ERP systems are no longer just systems of record. They are becoming the foundation for autonomous AI agents that can act, decide and coordinate across an entire organization.

## The shift

Traditional ERP implementation focuses on data entry and reporting. AI-first ERP flips this: the system becomes a source of truth that agents can query, reason over and act upon.

## What this means in practice

An AI agent connected to Dynamics 365 can:
- Close a period without manual intervention
- Flag variance anomalies before a human notices
- Coordinate approval workflows autonomously

## The bottleneck is not the AI

The biggest obstacle is not the language model. It is the data model. Organizations with clean, canonical data in Dataverse are ready for agents today. Those without it are not.
